Wifi

Visitors who bring laptops with wireless cards can use the eduroam network, or request a temporary wireless account from UNL. For the latter option, just connect to the UNL-Guest network, open a browser, and follow the directions.

Dining

There are many restaurants within walking distance in the downtown area for lunch and dinner as well as the Nebraska Union. A list of restaurants will be available at the registration desk.

Additional local information can be found at the Lincoln Convention and Visitor's Bureau and Downtown Lincoln Association.
