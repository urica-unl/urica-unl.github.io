Registration Information

If you plan to attend just URiCA or both URiCA and KUMUNU, please register here, and indicate which conferences you plan to attend. Once you've completed that registration form you do not need to also complete the KUMUNU registration form. The only graduate students that need to complete KUMUNU registration are the graduate students that will be attending KUMUNU and will not be attending URiCA.

There is a block of hotel rooms reserved for those who register by April 10th. Do not call the hotel to make your room reservation as we will be providing a rooming list to the hotel directly. We will be able to provide mileage reimbursement for those traveling by car. For support with airfare, please email one of the organizers. We may still be able to reimburse some lodging or travel if you need to register after the deadline.
